Welcome to WeCare-mind.com!

WeCare-mind.com uses machine learning to analyze text entered by users and predicts the kind of disorder that could be affecting them.
Currently the disorders that we predict are as follows:
  • ADHD
  • PTSD
  • Depression
We are expanding our model to predict other disorders such as Bipolar Disorder, Postpartum Depression, and other Stress and Anxiety related conditions.

What problems do you face?

Our model uses your response to the above question to predict the mental health issue that's most likely associated with it. We also provide a list of online coping resources for the predicted disorder.

Since we aren't licensed practitioners, our feedback should be taken with a grain of salt and should not be considered a substitute for advice from actual healthcare practitioners.

About us

This application was developed by a group of students from the University of Massachusetts Amherst, New York Unversity and the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign.
The team members are Chetan Manjesh, Yu-Han Chen, Ayush Sharma, Suprith Aireddy and Shi Zhang.
We are very passionate about the area of mental health and are aware of the challenges that people sufferering from mental health related issues face. There is still a lot of stigma associated with mental illness that prevents people from reaching out to others, for this reason we created this website to enable people to be heard and to guide them in the right direction.
